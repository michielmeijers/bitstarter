Playing golf is one of the greatest ways of spending a day with friends or business associates. You're out in the open, getting a bit of excercise, while chatting away.

But at the end of the day, you'd like to know who won, right? You'd like to know your score. Who did better than his average, or whether you improved since the last time you played this course. So, you start filling in your score manually in some kind of application. Every score, every put, every point. Stop!

Here's Qolfer: just upload a snapshot of your scorecard and we'll do the rest. By the time you've ordered your second round, Qolfer has added your score to your profile so you can compare your score with your friends, or brag about it on Facebook or Twitter. You can even check your progress.

There's more

So, what about this Qolfer? It's a new startup from the Netherlands. The founders are keen golf players and have lots of experience with online applications. If you're interested in this application, please let us know. You can support us by preordering the app or by sharing our story.
